My First Blockchain Transaction

Blockchain intro - your first blockchain transaction (on a testnet):
1. From your mobile phone, open website to create a test wallet instantly: http://dev.kulap.io/libra
2. Wait for presenter's QR code to be displayed on the screen (auction wallet)
3. Click on 'Send', and choose 'Allow' to enable browser's camera access, and scan the QR code on the presenter's screen. enter any amount you want and 'Confirm' the payment.
4. Done! you just made a payment transaction on a blockchain.


a. Auctioner's site https://libraauction.azurewebsites.net
b. Blockchain explorer http://librabrowser.io, paste your wallet address
